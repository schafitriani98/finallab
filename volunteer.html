Volunteer Programme

Volunteers from all over the world are welcomed to join our conservation effort to help these sun bears

It is an experience not to be missed!

Activities

Animal Husbandry

This involves cleaning cages and feeding. You will work with different sun bears depending on the needs of the bears, keepers or the centre.

Enrichment

The idea of enrichment is to stimulate the animals and improve their living conditions as much as possible to mimic their natural habitat.

Sun Bear Observation

Gain fieldwork experience in the tropical rainforest environment and involve in rehabilitation process.

Construction

You may be asked to design and work on small construction projects to improve the night dens or enclosures.

Education

There is an education centre and library at BSBCC. Volunteers should learn as much as possible about the sun bears. You may be asked to talk to school children and design an education session for them.

Application Procedure

Step 1:

Enquiry form

Find out about the availability of a programme by filling out an Enquiry Form.

Step 2:

Application Form

Once we have confirmed that your selected programme and dates are available, we will send you an application form. This form will ask for your personal details, including experience and health conditions.

Step 3:

Skype Interview

Upon approval of application, you will be given a date and time for a Skype interview to be conducted. If you pass this interview, we will send you a confirmation and acceptance to be a volunteer. You will then be given 2 weeks to make the necessary payment.
